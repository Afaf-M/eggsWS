Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add milk, salt, and black pepper to the bowl.
  3. Beat the mixture with a fork until well combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and melt the butter.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.
  6. Gently stir the eggs as they cook to scramble them.
  7. Cook until the eggs are set but still moist.
  8. Remove the skillet from the heat.
  9. Serve the scrambled eggs hot and enjoy!

Serving Suggestion:

  1. Scrambled eggs go well with toast or bagels.
  2. For a complete breakfast, serve with some fresh fruits on the side.
