Sunny Side Up Egg Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over low heat.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to the skillet and let it melt.
  3. Crack one egg gently into the skillet, keeping the yolk intact.
  4. Season the egg with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook the egg on low heat until the whites are set, and the yolk is still runny. This should take about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Remove the sunny side up egg from the skillet and place it on a plate.
  7. Repeat steps 2 to 6 for the second egg.
  8. Serve the sunny side up eggs hot and enjoy!
