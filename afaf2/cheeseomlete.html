Cheese Omelet Recipe

Ingredients:

4 eggs
Whisked in a bowl.
1/4 cup onions
Finely chopped.
1-2 green chilies
Deseeded and finely chopped.
1/4 cup tomatoes
Diced.
1/4 cup cheese
Grated. You can use cheddar or any other type you prefer.

Instructions:

  1. In a non-stick skillet, heat some oil or butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  4. Pour the whisked eggs into the skillet.
  5. Let the eggs cook for a minute or so, then sprinkle the grated cheese on one half of the omelet.
  6. Once the cheese starts to melt, fold the omelet in half.
  7. Cook for another minute to ensure the cheese is fully melted.
  8. Slide the omelet onto a plate, cut it in half, and serve hot.

Serving Suggestion:

This simple omelet goes well with a side of toast or a fresh green salad.
